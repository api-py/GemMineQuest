# GemMineQuest -- App Store Description

## Subtitle
Match gems. Mine Wales. Seek the Dragon.

## Description

Descend into the legendary mines of Wales in this match-3 puzzle adventure inspired by 4,000 years of Welsh mining heritage and Celtic mythology.

Guided by the Coblynau -- mischievous mine spirits from Welsh folklore -- you'll match Welsh minerals, clear ancient blockers, and dig through 7 real mining regions of Wales. From the Bronze Age copper tunnels of the Great Orme to the dragon's lair beneath Dinas Emrys, every level brings you closer to Y Ddraig Goch -- the sleeping Red Dragon.

SEVEN WELSH MINING ZONES
- Great Orme: 4,000-year-old prehistoric copper mines
- South Wales Coalfields: The black gold of the Valleys
- Parys Mountain: The vivid Copper Kingdom of Anglesey
- Llechwedd Slate: UNESCO World Heritage caverns
- Dolgellau Gold Belt: Source of Royal wedding ring gold
- Dolaucothi Roman Mines: 2,000 years of ancient power
- Dinas Emrys: The mythical Dragon's Lair

WELSH MYTHOLOGY WOVEN IN
- Coblynau mine spirit companion guides your journey
- Ceridwen's Cauldron, Cwn Annwn (Otherworld Hounds), and Dragon Fire boosters
- Druid progression: rise from Ovate to Bard to Druid
- Annwn -- the deeper you dig, the more wondrous the world becomes

MATCH-3 MASTERY
- 6 unique Welsh mineral gems with stunning art
- 5 special gems with explosive chain reactions
- 6 mythical blockers: Slate, Bluestone, Iron Cage, Dragon Fire, Awen Crystal, and more
- 200+ procedurally generated levels with increasing challenge
- Worm mechanic, shuffle levels, and solvability guarantees

CELTIC ART & CULTURE
- Celtic knotwork borders and triskele motifs
- Love spoon star ratings -- traditional Welsh carved symbols
- 229+ real Welsh place names with map locations
- Full Welsh language (Cymraeg) support
